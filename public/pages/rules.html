Warzone Rules

Failure to follow any of the rules listed on this page will result in a punishment on your Minecraft or Discord account. If you have any questions if whether or not something is allowed, please contact a moderator for more information.

General Guidelines

  1. Maintain a casual and positive atmosphere at all times. Be respectful of all players. Harassment and bullying are strictly not allowed.
  2. Be kind to other players. While we're pretty lenient when it comes to swearing, we ask that you do not use foul language directed towards other players.
  3. No advertising of any kind. This includes Minehut servers, external servers, YouTube channels, etc.
  4. This is not a market. To protect our users from scams and other financial offenses, selling, soliciting, and/or buying anything through our services is not allowed.
  5. Chat spamming/flooding is not allowed. Additionally, keep everything as appropriate as you can.
  6. Any client modifications that may give you an advantage over other users while in game is not permitted. Mods such as 5zig, Labymod, Optifine, or any other similar mod which doesn't give yourself a major advantage over others but may affect your client visually is allowed.
  7. Be respectful to staff members. If they say something, you must listen and abide.
  8. Do not encourage other players to break rules. If a player breaks a rule in response to your directions, you will be held accountable.
  9. Do not impersonate staff members on any service, by rank, association, or username.
  10. Directed threats to players or the server are not permitted. This includes things under the scope of DDoS threats, Dox threats, death threats, or personal threats.

Gameplay Guidelines

  1. Due to the fact that we welcome all audiences, we require you to keep your username and Minecraft skin appropriate at all times, being caught with an inappropriate skin or username will result in a punishment when can be appealed for once the username or skin has been changed to suit all audiences.
  2. Doing anything that affects how your team would play during the match, such as killing your teammate via various different actions, letting the other team break your objective whilst you stand there and purposely refrain from contributing to the defense (also known as game throwing) and blocking the spawn of your team is completely prohibited/
  3. Any scenario of abusing statistics will not be tolerated, this includes; killing your alternet account numerous times to retrieve a large quantity of kills, leaving the game while your wool is being broken, abusing your manual join perk by joining the winning team when the objective is being broken, and disconnecting whilst in combat or while falling into the void.
  4. Usage of a blacklisted modification or automated combat software will not be allowed, this includes; macros, autoclickers, hacked clients, injected ghost clients and most importantly butterfly clicking as it allows you to click more than once (also known as double clicking
  5. If caught informing a member of another team that somebody is about to complete the objective, or providing the location of a player, you will be punished accordingly as ghosting is not allowed.

Discord Guidelines

  1. Refrain from the usage of derogatory / discriminatory terminology
  2. Don't be toxic, don't be generally hateful and disrespectful towards other users of the Discord.
  3. Flooding or spamming the channel with useless messages will not be tolerated and you will be punished for it.
  4. Avoid posting sexually explicit content, changing your nickname to a sexually explicit username, or being inappropriate at all.
  5. Leaking personal information, threatening users of the server and doing anything to cause a user emotional distress and anxiety will cause you to be banned, and we get your Discord account terminated.
  6. Causing users to not feel welcome here is not allowed, please respect the fact that this Discord is a relaxed and welcoming community-based server.

Please note that if you are IP banned in game or on our Discord server, all users connected to that same IP will also be banned. Additionally, these rules are subject to change at any time, without notice. Please check this page regularly for updates.

If you wish to plead guilty to your punishment and request to be unbanned you may appeal. If you believe that your punishment was incorrect and you would like to dispute your punishment, you may also appeal. If you would like to issue a player report, please post in our Discord to the relevant channel.
